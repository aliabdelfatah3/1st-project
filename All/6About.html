SALTA3 BURGER ABOUT:

We at Salta3 Burger believe that with quality comes success.

This formula is one we tested and tried. We strived for perfection, right

from the beginning. We set goals and promised ourselves that our

costumers weren’t only going to taste the best burgers, snacks, and

side dishes in town, but they were also going to receive the best service and experience.

Today we can proudly say that what we hoped for and aimed at from the very beginning, has been achieved. The happy faces and compliments we get from customers is what makesus want to work harder.

In 2016, we opened our first Salta3 Burger.

The restaurant was a humble burger spot, located in Nasr City, near the district’s most popular places. But soon enough the place couldn’t keep up with people’s demand and hunger for more.

August 2017 is when our second pearl was born. We chose Patio Mall in New Cairo as the location for our second branch.

We did this to ensure our customers in New Cairo wouldn’t have to go far to experience the burger sensation everyone in town is talking about.

The Company continued expanding our brand to open in El Sheikh Zayed in June 2018 and in Dokki in April 2019.

We are proud of our success and achievements, and we will make sure to keep our standards highs to guarantee our customer’s satisfsaction.
